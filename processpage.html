Main ideal of Website: “Life as Adventure"

First, I name and give a topic for my website “Life as Adventure", which makes sure the theme of the portfolio is around the path of my lifetime. Under this situation, I can design many different categories of the inner pages to show all the works and abilities and experiences I own in my life. It is the website that can show off my life experience and work portfolio.

Description

Since I was very young, I have been a desire to see more scenes and learn more abilities around the world. I want to explore every corner and knowledge of this world. When I start to grow up, being A new media or multiple media artist is my dream career. In this project, I focus to create my personal website that shows my path to growth as a multiple media artist. The goal of this website is to exhibit my personal style works and abilities in the online gallery and pages and shows my personal brand. And I will upload my latest work and experience on it. I hope I can narrate a personal brand that loves travel, painting, photography, drawing, computing and design. But also, I want to create works that have a special calm and wild style to show the scenes of the true world and digital world. I want to use the website to let the public see my works and increase my personal influence. Also, I want to get help from colleagues and more job opportunities related to multiple media. In conclusion, I want to create a stage to show off my personality and my personal brand and abilities and background.

Navgiation design

Because there are too much different types of information that need to show. So I follow the principle of disclosure to classification. The navigation has been designed to have "Explore" and "Gallery" 2 main parts which used to show the information and the works. "Explore" has "latest", "about me" and "abilities and honours", which is all about the information about me. by following the rule of time I put the latest page the first, and then the about me page and the honours page. "Gallery" also has 2 inner pages which are "photos" and "more works". I put series works in Photo and other classification works in more works, which is clear to explore and see. The footer has some basic options, except the privacy policy and donate. users and jump to the latest pages by clicking newsletter and contact page by clicking contact. It gives them more possibility to jump to other pages.

Contact page

By following the principle of disclosure, I don't put too much content when users first come into my pages, I just give a title and a simple description. So, this website can give them a clear and comfortable feel. But, I have set up a scrolling sign at the bottom of the page, which mentioned to users there is more to explore if they scroll down. Once they scroll down, they will see examples of some main inner pages, by following the principle of examples. At the first, they will see the latest content, which is the most recent. then they will see a slide of works and finally they will see the about me content. I use different parts to separate them, and all of them have the button "explore more" under it, users can freely choose to use the button to explore or use the navigation bar at the top. In each inner page, I design the same starting to maintain the style, But the format of content has been changed to fit the different types of information,
Such as the latest page I choose to use one card to take a whole col, and users must scroll down to see old ones, which emphasized the time from latest to oldest. Each card has a top part of the video, which is an example related to the content to help users better understand.
About me page is a full-text page, I put My picture at the top and the slogan, which is also an example of the article below, so users can know what I will say below and choose to read or not. The abilities page has the format of the list which shows all my abilities and experiences and information.
The photo pages show photos of different series. By following the principle of disclosure and example, I only put one photo as an example with some text to describe each series and hid the other photos. If users are interested in this series they can click to open and check all works belonging to this series, once they are so interesting and want to see the details, they can click the images and make them larger on their screen and it will become a slide page. at the bottom, I also hid too much content but I put a button to explore more.
The page of more work is a simple gallery list that has 3 works in a line, visitors can click to zoom out and watch it. Also, the button explores more been put at the bottom for further reading.
The page of contact has two tabs of contact and social, users can choose the Mr. or ms. in the form and their name and basic information and send it to me if they want they can follow me at the social page.

Overall

The main colour of this object I choose to use dark blue to create a feeling of quiet and emptiness. And choose white to be the letter color to increase the contrast with the background. I want this website to have a simple, easy style that lets users can easily find what they want. But also, not too simple that looks very unorganized. In typography, I choose to emphasize the topic. There are different background colour to divide the area to make the web looks more organized and professional. Also, for the gallery part. I put frames under every picture, which makes them clearer and easier to watch. white-spacing is very important to my project, If the web is too full, the feeling of quiet and empty will be destroyed, but if it is too much white-spacing there, the whole page will look unfinished, and hard to read, Logo: The logo is a combination of the words” Life as Adventure” and a simple line frame. Visual elements

To clearly divide each part of the content, I use a translucent background for the background of each part. This background can give users a better reading experience without breaking the feeling of emptiness and quiet.

This background image has a lot of dark blue area which takes up 2/3 of the whole window. Which makes the feel empty and quiet. The dark background also has a high contrast with white words. Also, because the bottom part is lighter, so users will want to slide down to make content remain in dark areas, which will encourage them to slide down and keep reading without mentioning it.

For some option buttons, I decided to remove the solid colour but instead, I use shadow to show it. This design is more fit into this website and will create a feeling of lightness for users. The use of shadow also creates a feeling of 3D or Layering of this website, which create a naked eye 3d effect.

For many frames or boxes, I totally remove the solid fill colour but only keep the Dividing line to separate areas, It is a minimalist design, which is clean, simple and easy to see.

Except follwing the principle of disclosure to design. I also design a page that shows all works and content at one time, which is the "More work" page. In these pages, works have been showing up without classification. It is a page for users freely find their best work. And it skips lots of actions such as click, which means they can fast review all of those works by simply scrolling down.

Some information buttons have also been designed, which can avoid users can not understand some professional terms such as CCIT or FAH, Also, it has the function to show some basic information about me. The colour of the button I choose to use is the INFO colour.

Overall

Accessibility and usability are very important to get as many people as possible to successfully browse the web and use it effectively. This web has followed the design rule of the WCAG 2.0 Guidelines and has been reaching AA level at most parts in the completed part.text alternatives have been providing for all non-text content and reference of the whole image is included in inline code comments.

Accessibility and usability

Although the background is an image which causes some difficulty to read, most parts of the text reach the Contrast Ratio of 5.49:1, which is past the WCAG AA level. In some areas, normal text is failed to pass the AAA level test. So, for some text-rich pages, I increase the size of the text to large text to make it readable.

The navigation bar has a fixed position on the top which promise its visuality and consistency. It has a light hover when users move the mouse on it, and when users click on the page, it will highlight to tell users where they are. Dropdown functions have also been used to create a clear and useful navigation, which won't confuse users.

Because of the use of Bootstrap, the whole website has perfect features of adaptable content. It means all content will change the size and format depending on the size of the windows to the best way to show. The navigation bar will change to a button that hides all options inside of it.

this scroll button perfectly solves the problem that owning a clear and simple home page and rich content home page together. Users would not see any content when they first come to this website, but the scroll button mentioned there is more to explore below. So, users won't confuse by complex content when they first come. By following the guidelines of the scroll button, they can understand this website step by step.

The "explore more" buttons on the home page give users more possibility to reach other pages without a navigation bar.

The back-to-top button in some text-rich pages allows users the ability to fast go back to the top.
