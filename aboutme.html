About Me

Male, 24 years old, who studied at the University of Toronto Mississauga in CCIT and FAH major. Interesting in painting, photography, drawing, computing and design.

Life is like an adventure to me, which have to complete missions and face to unknown future. I decided to design this website as a form of a portfolio which shows works and achievements along the way. By using this method, the memory and story will be recorded.

More About Me

Tianyu, I is a 24 years university student who studies at University with and programs. And also a boy who desires adventure in life. Since he was young, he has had the dream to explore this wonderful world. But too many life troubles stop his step. Now, Tianyu has started to pick up the camera pen to record his path and adventures in his study and around the world. In future, Tianyu prepare to participate in the photograph circle and art circle to show people his work that combines wild and empty.

Also, along with the path to becoming a travel photographer, I keep studying all my knowledge about design and art to increase my taste and ability to capture beauty. Also, I have studied piano for over 7 years, which gives me a good taste in music too.

CCIT
Communication, Culture, Information and Technology (CCIT) focuses on the generation, diffusion, and social impact of new technologies, and how media and technologies interact with commerce, culture and communication. The CCIT program combines academic courses in the arts and social sciences with hands-on applied courses in digital media and technology taught at UTM (https://www.utm.utoronto.ca/iccit/ccit, 2021)
FAH
The UTM and Sheridan joint Art & Art History program is the longest standing partnership of its type in Canada. It combines the study of art history at UTM with studio art courses at Sheridan Institute. Incoming students are enrolled immediately in a studio arts curriculum. The program offers six core studios that students complete in their initial two years of study: drawing, painting, sculpture/installation, print media, design and photography.(https://www.utm.utoronto.ca/dvs/programs/art-art-history, 2021)
